Top Fruits for Health

Eating fruits daily provides essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants for a healthy lifestyle.

  • Apple - Rich in fiber and supports heart health.
  • Orange - High in vitamin C and boosts immunity.
  • Strawberry - Packed with antioxidants and improves skin health.
  • Banana - Great source of potassium and energy.

Exotic Fruits Benefits

Exotic fruits bring unique flavors and nutritional benefits to your diet.

  • Mango - Boosts immunity and aids digestion.
  • Papaya - Supports digestive health and skin glow.
  • Pineapple - Anti-inflammatory and rich in vitamin C.
  • Dragon Fruit - High in fiber and antioxidants.

Fruits for Kids

Fruits are a fun and healthy snack for children.

  • Grapes - Sweet, juicy, and full of antioxidants.
  • Watermelon - Hydrating and rich in vitamins.
  • Blueberries - Small but packed with nutrients.
  • Banana - Easy to eat and energy-boosting.
